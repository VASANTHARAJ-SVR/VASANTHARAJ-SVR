<!-- ── ABOUT ME ─────────────────────────────────────────────── -->

## 🧠 About Me

I didn't start Neo Perion to write better code.  
I started it because I saw a gap — **most businesses understood their problems but had no way to translate them into technology that actually worked.**

While most people in my space were building portfolio projects, I was studying customer pain points, market dynamics, and business systems. That shift in thinking — from *engineer* to *product founder* — is what shaped everything I've built since.

Today, I run an **AI-first software company** that takes ideas from napkin sketch to production-ready digital product. My work sits at the intersection of software engineering, applied AI, and business execution.

**What drives me:**

- 🎯 Technology is only valuable when it creates real outcomes
- ⚙️ Execution separates ideas from companies
- 🏗️ Systems thinking over quick fixes
- 📈 Long-term value creation over short-term wins

## 💭 Founder Philosophy

<div align="center">

*Six principles that guide how I build:*

</div>

| # | Principle | What It Means in Practice |
